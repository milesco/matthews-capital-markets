**Subject:** Texas hotels: 25 bps tighter, Austin leads

Jon, Mark,

Texas hotel cap rates compressed 25 to 50 basis points off the 2024 peak through Q1 2026. Austin stabilized select-service now prices in the 7.50 to 8.25 percent band, the tightest in the state outside Hill Country resort. Hill Country resort itself is in the 6.50 to 7.75 percent range, anchored by the lifestyle bid that just showed up on our Walden Retreats Hill Country close.

For Bisnow Texas, the local angle: the Texas Triangle is now leading the national Sun Belt cap rate compression story rather than tracking it. Construction-loan freeze of late 2022 to early 2025 interrupted ground-breakings; net new select-service supply runs 1 to 3 percent through 2027 across Austin, DFW, Houston, and San Antonio, well below the 3 to 4 percent long-term average. Constrained supply plus tightened CMBS spreads plus a bid pool that has accepted current pricing is the formula.

For the national Bisnow desk: the same dynamics are visible across Phoenix, Nashville, Charleston, Greenville, and Tampa. Texas is the cleanest data set because the supply-pipeline read is least noisy.

Pitch for The Hotel Investor Playbook.

Three episode angles:

1. From listing to close on a Sun Belt lifestyle resort. The Walden Retreats walk-through. Buyer pool, cap rate band, broker-led dynamics, what the financing looked like, what blew up and what survived in diligence. This is the episode your investor listeners would forward.

2. How to read a 2026 hotel cap rate band by sub-market. Texas Triangle stabilized select-service is in the 7.50 to 8.75 percent range, full-service primary metro at 7.00 to 8.00 percent, tertiary-CBD full-service at 9.00 to 10.50 percent.
